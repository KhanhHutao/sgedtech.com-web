## Product Summary

SEMS helps education centers consolidate operational data that is often split across spreadsheets, chat apps, and paper workflows. The platform supports center operations through:

- Admissions and lead management
- Student and parent records
- Class schedules and attendance
- Tuition, payments, and finance reporting
- Teacher tracking and lesson reporting
- Parent and student portal access
- In-app guidance and AI-assisted help
